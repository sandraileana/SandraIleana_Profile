About us.
The National Cancer Institute Brachytherapy Division is one of the most experienced and advanced centers for intracavitary gynecologic brachytherapy (ICGBT) in the Mexico.
It provides ICGBT mainly for cervical cancer, followed by endometrial cancer.
It offers high-dose and low-dose rate ICGBT.
